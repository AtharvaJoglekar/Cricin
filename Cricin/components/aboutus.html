About Us

CricIn is a modern cricket news app recently launched in the market.Available on android as well as ios. It features news, articles and live coverage of cricket matches including videos, scorecards, text commentary, player stats and team rankings.

History

CricIn App was launched on 1st January 2020.It was the most talked about app at the start of year 2020.Reaching 10M downloads in just 2 months.It got the best newcomer app award in march 2020.
